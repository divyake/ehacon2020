About Conference

eHaCON2020, the 3rdeHaCON in the series, brings together computer security researchers, cryptographers, industry representatives, graduate students and hackers interested in any aspect of information security and privacy. eHaCON 2020represents a balanced endeavor consisting of 3-minute Scholar Thesis Presentations, Live demonstrations, Workshops and Coding competitions on hacking. The most substantial new findings about computer network attacks and defenses, commercial security solutions, and pragmatic real-world security experience will be presented in a four-day series of informative workshops and invited talks. The general chair along with the entire team invites researchers and hackers around the world to take part in the upcoming event. .
