About Conference

eHaCON-2020, the 3rd eHaCON in the series, brings together computer security researchers, cryptographers, industry representatives, graduate students and hackers interested in any aspect of information security and privacy. eHaCON 2020 represents a balanced endeavour consisting of 3-minute Scholarly Thesis Presentations, Live demonstrations, Workshops and Coding competitions on hacking. The most substantial new findings about computer network attacks and defenses, commercial security solutions, and pragmatic real-world security experience will be presented in a four-day series of informative workshops and invited talks. The general chair along with the entire team invites researchers and hackers around the world to take part in the upcoming event. .

Three Minutes Scholarly Thesis (3MST) is a competition that challenges B.Tech, M.Tech & Ph. D students to present a convincing oration on their thesis and its significance in just 3 minutes, in a clear language. 3MST requires candidates to combine their ideas of research discoveries and capture the imagination of listeners.

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.Tech Students
  • M.Tech Students
  • Ph.D Students


Rules

  • Only one single static PowerPoint slide is permitted. (No slide transitions, animations or ‘movement’ of any description. The slide is to be presented from the beginning of the proclamation.
  • No additional electronic media (e.g. sound & video files) are permitted.
  • No additional props (e.g. costumes, musical instruments, laboratory equipment) are permitted.
  • Presentations are restricted to 3 minutes duration.
  • Presentations are to be spoken word.
  • Presentations are to be considered to have commenced when a presenter starts their presentation through movement or speech.
  • The decision of the adjudicating panel is final.
